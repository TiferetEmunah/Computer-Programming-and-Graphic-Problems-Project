Welcome to Archinspire!


This is a website dedicated to providing a place where the design community can review different kinds of work made by the students and professional as well, allowing for greater connection between the two. Our goal is to provide a forum of inspiration for those who want to see what others have done, and create an overall collaborative practice of architectural design.


Examples provided in these galleries can apply to more than just architects and architecture students! They can also serve as inspiration for:

  1. Interior, industrial and digital design students;
  2. Professional designers and developers;
  3. Teachers of design disciplines;
  4. And those interested in design.
